5.2.3 The Height Plot

Figure 5-8 shows the height of the antenna as calculated by the receiver over the 24 hr data collection period. Due to the geometry of the satellite constellations, the calculated position of an antenna on earth will always show the largest error in the vertical component. If any problems are suspected in positioning they will be most evident in the height plot.
